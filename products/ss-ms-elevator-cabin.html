SS / MS Elevator Cabin

Structural Integrity with Functional Design

We manufacture Stainless Steel (SS) and Mild Steel (MS) elevator cabins that combine structural integrity with functional design. Cabins are produced to customer-defined dimensions, finishes, and application requirements.

Subcategories

SS Cabin

Standard stainless steel cabins offering corrosion resistance and long service life.

SS Designer Cabin

Custom-finished stainless steel cabins developed to meet architectural and aesthetic requirements.

MS Cabin

Mild Steel cabins suitable for industrial, service, and utility-focused elevator applications.
