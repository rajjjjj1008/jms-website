COP / LOP

Durable Control Panel Enclosures for Elevator Systems

JMS Industries manufactures Car Operating Panels (COP) and Landing Operating Panels (LOP) as precision-fabricated mechanical panel assemblies used in elevator systems. These panels form the structural and aesthetic interface for elevator controls, ensuring durability, accurate fitment, and long-term performance in high-usage environments.

Elevator Panels

Car Operating Panel (COP)

Car Operating Panels (COP) manufactured by JMS Industries are designed to be installed inside the elevator cabin, providing a robust and precisely fabricated enclosure for elevator control interfaces.

Landing Operating Panel (LOP)

Landing Operating Panels (LOP) manufactured by JMS Industries are mechanical panel assemblies installed at elevator landings. These panels are designed to match the architectural and functional requirements.
