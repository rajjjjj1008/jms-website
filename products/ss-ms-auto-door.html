Elevator Door system

Smooth Operation with Advanced Safety Features

JMS Industries manufactures automatic elevator door assemblies in stainless steel and mild steel, engineered to deliver smooth operation, durability, and integration into varied elevator systems. These doors are designed to withstand rigorous operational cycles while maintaining consistent performance over time.

Subcategories

Auto Door

JMS Industries manufactures an automatic elevator door and frame assemblies designed for smooth operation and long service life. These doors are suitable for passenger and commercial elevator applications where consistent operation and durability are essential.

SS Door and Frame

Manufactured in stainless steel and mild steel, these door and frame assemblies offer excellent corrosion resistance, clean aesthetics, and durability, making them suitable for residential and commercial elevators.

MS Powder coated Door and Frame

Robust mild-steel door and frame assemblies with a powder-coated finish, designed for strength, wear resistance, and reliable performance in high-usage environments.

Manual Door

Manual door and frame systems manufactured by JMS Industries are designed for functional reliability and structural strength, commonly used in service, utility, and specific passenger elevator applications.

Swing Door and Frame

Hinged swing door systems suitable for manual operation are commonly used in service elevators and low-traffic installations.

Collapsible Door and Frame

Compact, space-efficient collapsible door systems designed for utility and service elevators where functional access and durability are priorities.

Manual Telescopic Door and Frame

Manual telescopic door systems engineered for smooth sliding while optimizing landing space.
