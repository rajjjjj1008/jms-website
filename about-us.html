Our Story

JMS Industries is a trusted leader in the Indian elevator industry, with over 15 years of engineering excellence. We deliver premium end-to-end solutions, from precision manufacturing and seamless assembly to dedicated after-sales service. Every elevator is thoughtfully customized to enhance architectural vision while ensuring safety, reliability, and refined craftsmanship.

Our commitment to quality and innovation drives us to create mobility experiences that blend performance with design. Built on trust and exceptional service, JMS Industries continues to redefine vertical transportation, offering elevators that elevate lifestyles and set new benchmarks in reliability, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Commitment

JMS Industries is committed to being a reliable manufacturing partner to the elevator industry. We measure success not just by output, but by how well our components perform within our customers' systems, over time, across projects, and through repeated production cycles.

By combining experience, precision manufacturing, and a quality-first mindset, we continue to support the elevator industry with components engineered for long-lasting performance and trust.

Our Journey

  • Foundation

    JMS Industries was founded in 2009 under the leadership of Mr. K. Saravanan and Mr. M. Joseph.

    The company was established with a focused vision to manufacture mechanical components for the elevator industry.

    From inception, precision and reliability formed the foundation of every product and process.

  • Early Growth

    JMS Industries built strong capabilities in elevator car frames and safety-operated switches.

    The portfolio expanded to include car guide shoes, junction boxes, and other mechanical components.

    These capabilities supported elevator OEMs with build-to-print manufacturing solutions.

  • Capability Expansion

    The company expanded its product portfolio to include elevator cabins and elevator door systems.

    Additional offerings such as guide rail brackets and machine mounting components strengthened manufacturing depth.

    This phase marked JMS Industries‚Äô evolution into an end-to-end mechanical manufacturing partner.

  • Renowned Expertise

    JMS Industries earned strong goodwill in the elevator industry for high-quality elevator car frames.

    The company also became known for executing non-standard jobs tailored to unique customer requirements.

  • Present Day

    With over 15 years of industry experience, JMS Industries continues to deliver precision-engineered elevator components.

    The company is led by Mr. M. Joseph and Mr. Sriram Prasad Saravanan, representing the second generation of leadership.

    Today, JMS Industries remains committed to reliability, quality, and long-term customer trust.
